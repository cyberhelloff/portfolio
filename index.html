Results That Speak

Case Studies

01

Scaling Startup Event Registrations by 300%

Problem

A flagship startup conference was struggling with low registration numbers and poor-quality leads, relying solely on organic outreach and word-of-mouth.

→
Strategy

Designed a multi-channel acquisition funnel using Google Ads (search + display), Meta lead-gen ads targeting startup founders, and LinkedIn InMail campaigns to CXOs.

→
Execution

Launched UTM-tracked campaigns with A/B tested creatives, implemented retargeting pixels, and built a drip email sequence via Mailchimp for registered leads.

→
Results

1,200+ registrations (up from 300), 45% lower CPL, 22% attendee-to-lead conversion rate, and 3 new sponsor partnerships secured.

02

Building a 50+ Partner Ecosystem from Scratch

Problem

The organization had no established partnerships network, limiting reach, credibility, and co-marketing opportunities within the startup ecosystem.

→
Strategy

Created a tiered partnership framework targeting accelerators, incubators, academic institutions (IITs, IIMs), and corporates with tailored value propositions.

→
Execution

Executed 500+ personalized B2B outreach emails via Apollo.io, conducted 100+ pitch meetings, and structured MOU-based partnerships with clear deliverables.

→
Results

50+ active ecosystem partners, 100+ institutional collaborations, 3x community growth, and invitations to co-host events at ISB and BITS Pilani.

03

SEO-Driven Content Strategy for Organic Growth

Problem

The startup media platform was over-reliant on paid channels with virtually zero organic traffic, leading to unsustainable customer acquisition costs.

→
Strategy

Conducted keyword research via Ahrefs, built a content calendar targeting long-tail startup ecosystem keywords, and optimized on-page SEO with proper schema markup.

→
Execution

Published 40+ SEO-optimized articles, built 200+ backlinks through partner content exchanges, and set up Google Analytics 4 + GTM for granular content performance tracking.

→
Results

400% organic traffic growth in 6 months, 15 keywords on page 1, 60% reduction in CAC, and organic became the #1 acquisition channel.
